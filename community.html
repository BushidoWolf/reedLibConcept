SYBIL RIDES Author Meet and Greet

TUESDAY DECEMBER 11 AT 6:30 PM

Meet Author Larry Maxwell. Dr. Maxwell’s newly released book Sybil Rides, tells the inspiring true story of events leading to Sybil Ludington’s remarkable ride.

Find out why Benedict Arnold is one of the heroes in this story and meet people on both sides of the struggle for American independence.

For a holiday gift with local flair, consider a signed copy of Sybil Rides or one of the author’s other titles, all available for sale and signing.

Refreshments served. Registration requested. Please call the library to register, 845-225-2439.
